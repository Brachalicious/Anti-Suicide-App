Someone who listens wholeheartedly, letting the world fade away as they focus on you, and you alone. Someone who lets you pull off your “I’m fine” mask and hears what you’re saying, even when you don’t have all the right words to explain.

## WE ARE    NEVONIM

**We’re a surrogate family helping young adults in crisis through recovery and beyond.** Leveraging the wisdom won through our own life experiences of trauma, rehab, and recovery, we become our clients’ trusted confidantes and loyal guides.

Above the clinical and beyond the logistical, we are their **long-term advocate and safe space** as they make strides toward a life full of health and meaning.

## THE KEY TO A FULFILLING LIFE IS INSIDE OF YOU.

## HERE’S HOW WE’LL SUPPORT YOU WHEN YOU’RE READY.

## 1

## REACH OUT

Every healing process begins with a real conversation. On the other end of the phone is a regular guy or girl who understands— because we’ve been there ourselves.

## 2

## FIRST STEPS

We’ll evaluate your needs, either on the phone or in person, so we can connect you to the right case manager. We’ll also set you up with the best insurance, if applicable.

## 3

## JOURNEY ON

A blend of family, confidante, guide, and advocate, your case manager is here for you. From listening to you cry to finding the right therapist to bringing you food in rehab, they’ve got your back.

## 4

## STAND TALL

We never disappear. Your case manager will check in frequently, keep the conversation going, and give you a friendly shoulder to lean on. You’re not a case – you’re family.
